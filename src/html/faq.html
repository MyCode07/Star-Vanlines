How to check if a moving company is legitimate?

Before hiring a moving company, it’s important to make sure the mover is properly licensed and insured. It’s important to note that while interstate movers are required to register with the Federal government and can be found in the U.S. DOT system, local movers are only regulated by the state. Every state has their own licensing regulations, so you should read up on your state’s requirements to ensure that your local mover is legitimate.
