Submit a Claim

Thanks for allowing Star Vanlines to service your recent relocation. We offer a quick, convenient way to contact our claims adjustment team. For your convenience, we have provided an online version of our Claim Form to expedite your claim process. A receipt of your claim will be acknowledged and a claim adjuster will be assigned to your case. Your claim adjuster will review the claim and contact you with any further instructions or info regarding your domestic moving claim.

Since all damaged items are subject to inspection, please do not proceed with any repairs and do not dispose of any damaged items. A "comments" section has been provided at the end of the claim form should you need to provide further explanation for any items or issues referenced in your claim. First of all, you should know that you can file a complaint but only in the case where you have a signed inventory sheet. Of course, this is something that each and every professional moving company, such as Star Vanlines, will provide you with before the move.

One of the most important things you should do is to photograph all of your belongings before your movers transport them. An additional piece of advice would be to photograph your belongings before your mover takes them away. By doing this, you will have a piece of evidence that your items were in perfect condition before the movers loaded them in the moving truck. It is also a good idea to make a detailed inventory of your belongings. That will be of great use when you need to submit a claim.

Steps to resolve claims

At Star Vanlines we carry out a series of processes so that your claim is classified as it should and is resolved as soon as possible.

01

Start a claim

This process lasts from 1 to 3 days and in it, we offer you the option to submit a claim through an online form.

02

Revision

Takes place between day 1 and 12 since the claim is placed. We verify that we have the necessary information and if not, we ask you to send us the additional documentation.

03

Resolution

In the next 7 days, we review your claim and if it is rejected, we will inform you of the reason via email.

04

Compensation

If your claim is accepted, we will proceed to the corresponding payment. The installments may be affected by the form of payment, destination bank, holidays, etc.
