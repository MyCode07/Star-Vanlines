Your Rights and Responsibilities

At Star Vanlines we value our customers above any other van line. We handle and care for your belongings as if they were our own while in our hands. It is imperative that you thoroughly understand your rights and responsibilities when entrusting Star Vanlines with your valuable items. We also want you to be fully educated on your rights and responsibilities throughout your move with us. It is our belief that the most successful and efficient moves occur when you have full understanding of what your right and responsibilities are as the consumer. We have provided two links below reviewing the FMCSA publications. We encourage you to take the time to understand your rights and responsibilities when working with a professional mover like Star Vanlines.
